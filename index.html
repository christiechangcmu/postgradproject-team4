CMU Information Systems
Post Graduation Statistics

Learn more about statistics regarding post-graduates of the Information Systems program.

Overview

A majority of Information Systems students are employed after graduating from CMU. Some chose to continue education in a graduate program or are still exploring their employment options.

Employment Sources

Our students get their job offers from these ways:

Handshake

26% of post-graduate Information Systems students of Class of 2016 landed their full-time jobs by using Carnegie Mellon's career platform. Carnegie Mellon nows uses Handshake as the career platform (Previously called TartanTRAK).

Go to Handshake

Networking

19% of post-graduate Information Systems students of Class of 2016 received their full-time employment by the network they built. Students can connect and network with Alumni of the Information Systems Program by clicking the link provided below.

See Alumni Contact

Hired from Internship

32% of post-graduate Information Systems students of Class of 2016 received full-time return offers from their internships from the summer before.

View Intern Info

Job Fairs

16% of post-graduate Information Systems students of Class of 2016 got their full-time offers by attending one of Carnegie Mellon's job fairs. Carnegie Mellon hosts several job fairs throughout the academic year, including the Technical Opportunities Conference, Encompass, and Jumpstart.

Check CMU Job Fairs
